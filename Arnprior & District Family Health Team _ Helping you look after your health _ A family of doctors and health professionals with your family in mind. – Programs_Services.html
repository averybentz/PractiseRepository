ADFHT PROGRAMS AND SERVICES

At the Arnprior & District Family Health Team (ADFHT), we offer a variety of services and programs to meet the needs of our patients. In addition to the care that you receive from your family doctor, the ADFHT also has other health care providers available to support your needs. These additional programs and services are listed below. These programs support your health, by either helping to manage an existing disease or assisting in preventing future disease.

How to access our services

All of our programs and services are available to rostered patients.  If you are interested in joining any of our programs or receiving our services, you can self refer by calling our receptionist at 613 622 5763.  You can also ask your family physician or a member of our inter-professional health care team to send a referral to our receptionist.

Some of our programs are available to the general public, including:  Optifast Weight Management Program, Chronic Disease Self-Management, Healthy Heart, and Craving Change.  If you are interested in learning more or would like to joing one of these programs please call our receptionist.
